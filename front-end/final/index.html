BATTLE OF BOTS

Gear up for an exicting coding competition to
showcase your unique programming skills in

Dive In

The aim of the event is to create an Artificial Intelligence (bot) to play bob's online strategy game on your behalf. Imbue your bot with an excellent strategy, to help it in this battle for domination, against other bots in the arena!

Whether you are a beginning programmer or an expert, this is sure to be a great learning experience (with cool cash prizes!). Moreover it's a long challenge and you can take your time updating your submissions and review your bot's actions, live.

This is a team event of sizes 1 upto 3 members.
